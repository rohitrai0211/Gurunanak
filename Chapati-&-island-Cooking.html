SEMI AUTOMATIC CHAPATI MAKING MACHINE

A semi-automatic chapati making machine is a specialized piece of equipment designed to automate and streamline the process of making chapatis or flatbreads. This machine assists in the preparation of chapatis by automating certain tasks while still requiring some manual intervention.

A fully automatic chapati making machine is an advanced and efficient piece of equipment designed to automate the entire process of chapati or flatbread production. ISLAND COOKING RANGE

An island cooking range refers to a cooking appliance that is designed to be freestanding or installed as a centerpiece in the middle of a kitchen, typically on an island or a dedicated cooking area away from the walls. This type of cooking range allows for a more open kitchen layout, providing cooks with the flexibility to move around the appliance from all sides.
