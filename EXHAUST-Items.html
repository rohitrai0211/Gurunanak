An Air Handling Unit (AHU) is a central component of heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems. It is responsible for the circulation, filtration, heating, cooling, and sometimes humidification or dehumidification of air in a building or industrial facility. AHUs play a crucial role in maintaining indoor air quality and ensuring thermal comfort.
